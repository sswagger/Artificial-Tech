About Us!

We started out as a small company that no one knew about, but we thought we had a product that every company that ever used cloud based software could use. So, we hung in there, and proved to many that Open Circuits was something that they needed. Now, it is used by thousands around the world; and, some of our major customers impact tens of millions of people every year. AND... We even extended Open Circuits to be available to hobbyists and small scale companies.
