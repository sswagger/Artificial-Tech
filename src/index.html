Get Started

Blog

Want a website? but don't even know where to start? We've got you covered! The Blog subscription is a server that has web templates for you to start.

Hobby

This is for those people who know what they're doing, and don't mind messing up a few times. These servers have no pre-configuration, just an open playground!

Pro

Pro is for small businesses; it has same setup as a hobby subscription (which is to say "no pre-configuration"). This is perfect for a business that just needs an extra server.

Bigger than Free

Need something even bigger? We can do that too! Just shoot us an email, and we can work out a contract to provide whatever you need!
